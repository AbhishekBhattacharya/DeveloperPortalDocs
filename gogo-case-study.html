Gogo Case Study

What Was Built?

An inflight Paid TV service connecting inflight video rental and an on-land mobile-enabled video catch-up experience on Delta and American Airlines flights nationwide.

Why Kaltura?

  • Challenging Deployment
    Custom workflows, tight integrations and a need for a hybrid deployment of on- premise and cloud
  • Platform Flexibility
    APIs and SDKs that can be distributed via OEM 
  • Time to Market and Budget
    Needed to prove a new business model with a limited timeline

Background

Gogo is the provider of in-flight broadband Internet service across more than 12 airline partners including Delta, American, Air Canada, United, US Airways, Virgin America, Virgin Atlantic, Japan Airlines, and more
