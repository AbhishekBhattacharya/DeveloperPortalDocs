Toon Goggles Case Study

What Was Built?

A platform catering to children and animators. Animators can publish their shows and access analytics on trends that can be presented to networks and investors.

Why Kaltura?

  • Platform API and SDKs
    Custom workflows, tight integrations with 3rd party systems and native mobile apps
  • Player Toolkit
    Seamless playback across web browsers, social networks and native apps
  • Advanced Transcoding Workflows
    Delivery of video content across devices, including bespoke Android Tablets and Sharp Aquos TVs

Background

Toon Goggles is a COPPA compliant entertainment service for children offering animated cartoons, live-action shows, games and music via the web, mobile apps, devices, smart TVs and tablets. Toon Goggles is used by over 3M kids worldwide.
