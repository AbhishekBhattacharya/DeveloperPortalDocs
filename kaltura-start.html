KalturaStart

The KalturaStart program provides startups with the tools and infrastructure needed to build, integrate or scale video applications at low cost using Kaltura VPaaS. Kaltura powers some of the world's largest video businesses and many of the hottest video startups - including Vodafone, TMZ, Sesame Street and NewsBeat Social. Build your video applications on top of Kaltura VPaaS and join some of the fastest-growing startups in world.

FAQs

I didn't attend an incubator or accelerator

If you didn't attend an incubator or accelerator, please sign up for a regular VPaaS account here

My incubator or accelerator is not on your approved list

Good news! You may still be eligible for KalturaStart. Please have your incubator or accelerator reach out to us via email - community@kaltura.com

What does 'video is a core element of your product' mean?

You are building a video-centric application or you are integrating video into an application which has a non-video focus, but the video component is sizable

I've raised more than $3M in capital

Unfortunately you're not eligible for KalturaStart. Please sign up for a regular VPaaS account here

Unfortunately you're not eligible for KalturaStart. Please sign up for a regular VPaaS account here
