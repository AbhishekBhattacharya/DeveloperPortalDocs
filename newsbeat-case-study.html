Newsbeat Social Case Study

What Was Built?

An end-to-end video ingestion, management, publishing, distribution, syndication, monetization and analytics workflow

Why Kaltura?

  • Platform API and SDKs
    Custom workflows, tight integrations and native mobile apps to engage viewers
  • Player Toolkit
    Seamless playback across web browsers, social networks and native apps
  • Content Distribution Connectors
    The ability to centrally mangage and distribute content to social networks at scale
  • Ad Integrations
    Monetize content views out-the-gate, across multiple paltforms

Background

Newsbeat Social is a video-first news agency, gathering and delivering one-minute fact-intensive original news reports. Newsbeat reaches 5M+ video views from 50 new video releases per day

"Kaltura provided us with flexible and future-proof video solutions to fit our needs. It includes the full range of video tools and capabilities, such as streaming and delivery on all devices, transcoding, analytics and support and maintenance services. We leverage Kaltura for our video ingestion, management, publishing, distribution, syndication, monetization and analytics."
